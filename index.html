About

A fifth year software engineer with a passion for teamwork and technology.

Hi, I'm Scott, a 23-year old, a Program Manager on the Web Platform Team at Microsoft in Redmond, WA and a Software Engineering graduate of the University of Victoria in Victoria, B.C. I enjoy dabbling in all sorts of things including technology, food, photography and blogging.

I am passionate about software development, with a focus on mobile and web application development, and I continuously strive to apply my knowledge of Software Engineering to create innovative software both in school and in the workplace. While working on team projects, I also seek to create a strong group dynamic through faciliticating communication and ensuring that all group members are working towards a shared, and mutually agreed upon goal.

Throughout my various work terms as part of the co-op program at UVic, I have been fortunate enough to work in a variety of positions at both large and small companies. Most recently as a Program Manager at Microsoft, I discovered that I enjoy and excel at collaborating with customers and teams of various sizes to turn a vision into a useful product.

Most recently, I have been working hard with a team of students at UVic to build beLocal, a web application aimed to connect local farmers and foodmakers with the community. We're still in the early phases of getting off the ground, but working on this project has taught me so much about running a startup and working with modern web frameworks such as AngularJS, Django, and Twitter Bootstrap.

Outside of work, I go to the gym, and enjoy listening to music, taking photos, and hiking. All things food and coffee related are also close to my heart, so I love exploring any city for new places to eat, and learning about new dishes to try at home.
