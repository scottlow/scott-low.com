About Me

When I was young, I knew that in addition to being interested in technology, I wanted to do something that had the potential to positively impact the lives of as many people as possible. After realizing this, the things I did growing up, ranging from teaching kids how to build games in Adobe Flash to choosing Software Engineering as my major, reflected my desire to feed my curiosity for technology and to combine it with my passion for collaborating with others. Throughout my internships and design projects in college, I quickly realized that I enjoyed learning about, and putting myself into the shoes of customers; this was something I did then, and still do today in order to design and build both products and experiences that delight users.

I'm currently a Program Manager at Microsoft working on the Microsoft Edge extensions platform. This role lets me continue the journey I started as a kid by combining my technical knowledge of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS with my love of building relationships and great experiences with customers and partners.

Outside of work, you'll find me dabbling in home automation, food, coffe, photography and far too many musical instruments.
