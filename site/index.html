About Me

When I was young, I knew that in addition to being interested in technology, I wanted to do something that had the potential to positively impact the lives of as many people as possible. This led me to pursue a degree in Software Engineering from the University of Victoria where I learned that I was drawn to learning deeply about users and collaborating with others to build products and experiences that filled customers' unmet needs.

I'm currently a Senior Program Manager working on privacy and trust on Microsoft Edge's HTML Platform team. I also lead the team of new hires that runs our team's "Squad", a diverse and inclusive community of early-in-career talent that aims to increase the satisfaction of new hires on the team by growing the size of their networks and creating a sense of belonging.
