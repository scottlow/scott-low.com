beLocal Victoria

beLocal Victoria is an application that aims to connect local farmers and food makers with their community. Since our team members are all foodies, we wanted to create a product that would help raise awareness about Victoria's rich local food scene. beLocal is the realization of that vision, allowing local farmers and food makers to connect with the community by posting company details, information about their products, and where they will be selling.
